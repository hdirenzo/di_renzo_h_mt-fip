Mericola's Pub Ale since 1853

Hey there! Mericola's Pub Ale has been brewed with the same formula since 1853 in Manchester.
Mericola's Original formula is made with a hint of pure honey, collected by local bee keepers.
Mericola's is brewed with all natural herbal essences.
